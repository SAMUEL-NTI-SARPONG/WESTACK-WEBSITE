Civil Engineering Services

Westack Engineering provides comprehensive civil engineering services for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects. Our team delivers quality solutions from site preparation through project completion, ensuring compliance with industry standards and client specifications.

  • Site Preparation: Earthworks including clearing, excavation, backfilling, and compaction
  • Infrastructure Construction: Roadworks, pavement construction, and building construction/rehabilitation
  • Specialized Foundations: Construction of concrete foundations for pipelines and equipment, pipe supports, sleepers, and anchor blocks
  • Drainage and Utilities: Design and construction of drainage systems, trenching for underground utilities, and construction of valve chambers and inspection pits
  • Concrete Works: Structural support, concrete works for industrial and commercial buildings
  • Environmental Services: Environmental rehabilitation and site restoration
