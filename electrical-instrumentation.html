Electrical & Instrumentation Services

Electrical & Instrumentation Engineering

West ACK Engineering provides comprehensive electrical and instrumentation engineering services for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects. Our qualified engineers and technicians deliver safe, reliable, and code-compliant solutions across power systems and automation.

  • High Voltage Systems: Construction of indoor and outdoor substations, transmission and distribution of power (161KV, 33KV, and 11KV)
  • Power Distribution: Low voltage (440v, 240v) distribution, power cable jointing and termination
  • Power Equipment: Installation of switchgears, transformers, MCCs, and power factor improvement plants
  • Backup and Generation: Supply and installation of diesel/gas generator sets, UPS systems (AC and DC), and auto changeover panels
  • Building Systems: Electrical wiring, data networks, CCTV, fire detection systems, and explosion-proof lighting
  • Automation and Control: Installation and commissioning of SCADA systems, DCS (Distributed Control Systems), and PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers)
  • Testing and Maintenance: Calibration, troubleshooting, and testing of instrumentation systems
