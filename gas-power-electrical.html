Gas, Power & Electrical Services

Electrical, Instrumentation & Natural Gas Engineering

West ACK Engineering provides comprehensive electrical, instrumentation, and natural gas engineering services for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects. Our qualified engineers and technicians deliver safe, reliable, and code-compliant solutions across power systems, automation, and gas facilities.

Electrical and Instrumentation Engineering

  • High Voltage Systems: Construction of indoor and outdoor substations, transmission and distribution of power (161KV, 33KV, and 11KV)
  • Power Distribution: Low voltage (440v, 240v) distribution, power cable jointing and termination
  • Power Equipment: Installation of switchgears, transformers, MCCs, and power factor improvement plants
  • Backup and Generation: Supply and installation of diesel/gas generator sets, UPS systems (AC and DC), and auto changeover panels
  • Building Systems: Electrical wiring, data networks, CCTV, fire detection systems, and explosion-proof lighting
  • Automation and Control: Installation and commissioning of SCADA systems, DCS (Distributed Control Systems), and PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers)
  • Testing and Maintenance: Calibration, troubleshooting, and testing of instrumentation systems

Natural Gas Engineering and Construction

  • Pipeline Services: Pre-commissioning, commissioning, and post-commissioning of natural gas pipelines
  • Facility Installation: Installation of flow and pressure regulating skids, filter separators, and fuel gas integration systems
  • Maintenance: Pigging of natural gas pipelines, inspection, calibration, and servicing of gas/oil flow metering
  • Protection: Installation of cathodic protection systems and calibration of detectors
