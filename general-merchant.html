General Merchant Services

Westack Engineering operates a dedicated procurement team that provides comprehensive general merchant services to support project execution and client needs. We leverage our network and expertise to deliver timely and cost-effective supply solutions.

  • Import and Export Management: Comprehensive management of international goods procurement, customs clearance, and logistics coordination
  • Local Supply Services: Purchase, supply, and distribution of materials and equipment with focus on fast response times (LEAD TIME)
  • Equipment Procurement: Sourcing and supply of industrial equipment, machinery, and tools
  • Materials Supply: Provision of construction materials, consumables, and spare parts
  • Vendor Management: Building and maintaining relationships with reliable suppliers and manufacturers
  • Quality Assurance: Ensuring all procured items meet specified quality standards and certifications
  • Inventory Management: Strategic stock management and just-in-time delivery services
  • Cost Optimization: Leveraging bulk purchasing and supplier networks to provide competitive pricing
